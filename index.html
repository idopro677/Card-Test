♠️ Features Guide

📷

Start / Stop Camera

Opens your rear camera and runs the YOLOv8 model on every frame to detect cards in real time. Colored boxes appear instantly around each card.

📸

Freeze & Analyze

Captures the current camera frame, stops the feed, and runs a full analysis — shows all detected cards with suit breakdown and saves to history.

⏳

Countdown Freeze

When ON, pressing Freeze shows a 3-2-1 countdown with audio ticks before capturing, giving you time to steady the camera.

📊

Live Stats Bar

Displays how many cards and distinct suits the AI currently sees, plus a running frame counter — great for knowing when all cards are in view.

🖼️

Upload Image

Pick a photo from your gallery. The AI instantly detects all cards, draws boxes color-coded by suit, and shows a breakdown panel below.

📜

Scan History

Thumbnails of your last 8 uploads. Each badge shows the card count detected. Tap a thumbnail to view it.

🔄

Reset

Clears the image, resets all session stats, and empties the history panel for a fresh session.

🎯

Confidence Slider

Sets the minimum score the AI needs before drawing a box. Lower = more detections. Higher = fewer but more accurate. Default 45%.

✨

Glow Boxes

Adds a neon glow around detection boxes, color-coded per suit: red for Hearts, orange for Diamonds, green for Clubs, blue for Spades.

🏷️

Labels

Shows the card name (e.g. "KH", "10D") on each bounding box. Toggle off to show boxes only.

🔢

Confidence %

Shows or hides the confidence score next to each label (e.g. "KH 91%").

🌙

Dark Mode

Switches to a dark theme — easier on the eyes in low-light conditions.

🔊

Sound

Plays a scan beep on detection and a victory chime when all cards are identified. Toggle to mute.
